Situation


There are an estimated 1.8 billion menstruating people around the world. In Mexico, according to the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (INEGI), we inhabit 63,356,944 women who represent 50% of the total population of the country. Women between the ages of 10 and 54 make up almost 60% of this population. The fertile life of an average woman is approximately four decades. However, even though menstruation is an unavoidable biological situation for half the population, menstrual hygiene products are not considered as necessary products.



Important facts


Each woman uses an average of 11,000 pads / tampons in her entire life. A feminine pad takes approximately 500 years to degrade. Approximately 5,750,000 towels end up in the trash or drains in Mexico each year
